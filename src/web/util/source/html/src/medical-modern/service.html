Neurology

Αt the Oxygenna Neurology Department, we treat hearing loss and related auditory problems in individuals of all ages. The clinic works in close cooperation with a variety of specialists to provide state of the art diagnostic, electrophysiologic, and balance testing for patients with any degree of hearing loss or communicative impairment.

A full-service dispensing program is available for hearing aid and assistive listening devices as well as rehabilitative services for hearing loss, dizziness and balance problems, tinnitus and hyperacusis.

Dr. John Langan

John Langan is the director of the Neurology Clinic. He provides comprehensive Neurology evaluations for adult and pediatric patients, specializing in vestibular and balance disorder.
