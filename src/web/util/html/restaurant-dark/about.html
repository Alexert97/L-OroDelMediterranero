Our restaurant

We would like to welcome you to our elegant restaurant for breakfast, lunch or evening meal. Our menu includes a range of delicious Dutch and international dishes. And if the weather permits, why not take a table on our spacious sun terrace.

A warm welcome awaits you in our restaurant every day. Our seasonal menu has an international flavour and offers a changing selection which includes fresh vegetables.

At breakfast we offer an extensive Live Cooking breakfast buffet. Choose from our freshly prepared omelettes and pancakes, for example, which are cooked to order by our chef. Our generous breakfast buffet also offers the choice of crispy croissants and freshly-baked bread rolls, scrambled eggs and bacon, as well lighter fare such as organic yoghurt and freshly-pressed fruit juices.

If there are sufficient reservations, you could decide to make your selection from the extensive range on our varied lunch buffet. The lavish buffet changes daily and offers a selection of hot and cold dishes, luxury breads, various desserts, cheeses and cold meats and fresh fruit. Coffee, tea, milk and fruit juice are included in the price.
