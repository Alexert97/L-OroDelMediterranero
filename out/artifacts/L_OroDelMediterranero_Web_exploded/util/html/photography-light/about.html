About Me *

Hello! I’m John Verso, a abstract and portrait photographer serving Greece and the main Europe. I’m so excited you’re here. Custom portraiture is about recording your surroundings – who you are and how you perceive yourself at any moment.

That’s what I strive to create and give every client – candid, lifestyle moments of their memories and loved ones. I want to showcase your joy and love of each other, so you can create beautiful wall displays to enjoy on a daily basis. My style is simple, natural photos that fit you, your family and the way you live.

After marrying my high school sweetheart in 2002, she gifted me a camera. I unwrapped the box, but what I didn’t realize then was that I also unwrapped my dreams. With little experience, no training, but a lot of heart, I set out to become a photographer…and that’s precisely what happened.

Since the inception of my business, I’ve photographed hundreds of weddings, traveled the world to document nuptials, made memories to last a lifetime, and voted Top 10 Wedding Photographers in the World by American Photo Magazine. For the first time in my life, I let my dreams steer me toward my hopes and it resulted in taking a risk I’m proud of.
