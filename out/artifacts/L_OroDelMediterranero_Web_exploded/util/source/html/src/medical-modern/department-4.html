Neurology

Welcome to the Department of Neurology at Oxygenna. We are caregivers, teachers and scholars who are dedicated to providing the very best neurological care, conducting world-class research and educating future generations of neurologists and neurological researchers.

The department offers cutting-edge diagnostic approaches, including advanced neuroimaging technologies, and access to trials of new medications and other therapeutic modalities.
