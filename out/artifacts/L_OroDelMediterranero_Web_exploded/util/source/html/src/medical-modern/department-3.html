Dental Care

The Dental Department focuses on oral health treatment and disease prevention. We strive to educate our community members on all their oral health needs and concerns to help you make decisions that are right for you.

We understand dental diseases can affect quality of life.

Dental

Services

We understand that going to the dentist can sometimes be intimidating. We would like our community members to know we want to support you and make your dental visit as comfortable as possible.

Below is a list of Adult and Child Services we offer but not limited to the following.

  • Oral Exams
  • Digital Radiography
  • Cleanings
  • Fillings
  • Oral Surgery
  • Denture fitments.
  • Partial denture fitments
  • Implants
  • Teeth Whitening
